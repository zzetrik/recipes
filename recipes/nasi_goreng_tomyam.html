Nasi Goreng Tomyam

Back to Recipes

Made by Xal.


Description

The Ingredients

  • What we need.
      3 tbsp. tomyam paste
      4 kaffir lime leaves, torn
      1 lemongrass, tender bottom bit, finely chopped
      2 chilli padi, chopped
      2 garlic, chopped
      1 yellow onion, chopped
      2 tbsp. oil
      1/2 cup squid, sliced
      1/2 cup chicken breast, cubes
      2 tsp. fish sauce
      3 cups cooked rice, preferably cold
      1 tsp. sugar

    • How to Make

    Nasi Goreng Tomyam Steps
    Uno Heat oil and sauté onions and garlic until soft and translucent. Add in chilli padi, lemongrass, and kaffir lime leaves. Sauté quickly until it becomes fragrant.
    Dos 2. Add in tom yum paste, chicken breast and squid. Mix well until chicken and squid is just cooked.
    Tres Pour in cooked rice and give it a good mix. Make sure rice is well coated in the sauce.
    Cuatro Season with fish sauce and sugar. Adjust seasoning to taste. You can keep it tangy or sweet.
