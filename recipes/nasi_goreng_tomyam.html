Nasi Goreng Tomyam

Back to Recipes

Description

The Ingredients

  • What we need.
      3 tbsp. tomyam paste
      4 kaffir lime leaves, torn
      1 lemongrass, tender bottom bit, finely chopped
      2 chilli padi, chopped
      2 garlic, chopped
      1 yellow onion, chopped
      2 tbsp. oil
      1/2 cup squid, sliced
      1/2 cup chicken breast, cubes
      2 tsp. fish sauce
      3 cups cooked rice, preferably cold
      1 tsp. sugar

    • How to Make

    Ice Lemon Tea Steps
    Uno Boil 2 cups of water and add sugar.
    Dos Then add tea dust and cover it for 2 minutes on low flame.
    Tres Squeeze lemon in glass directly and add ice cubes.Put Lemon slice on top of ice.
    Cuatro Pour tea in a glass and drizzle some honey on it.
    Cinco Decorate with mint and Serve it.
    Seis Enjoy!
