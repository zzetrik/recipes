Ice Lemon Tea

Back to Recipes

Description

The Ingredients

  • What we need.
      2 cup water
      1 tsp tea dust
      5 tsp Sugar
      3 lemon slices
      1 Lemon for juice
      1/2 tsp honey
      Mint leaves (optional- just for decoration)

    • How to Make

    Ice Lemon Tea Steps
    Uno Boil 2 cups of water and add sugar.
    Dos Then add tea dust and cover it for 2 minutes on low flame.
    Tres Squeeze lemon in glass directly and add ice cubes.Put Lemon slice on top of ice.
    Cuatro Pour tea in a glass and drizzle some honey on it.
    Cinco Decorate with mint and Serve it.
    Seis Enjoy!
